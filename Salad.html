Description

A fresh and vibrant mix of crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and seasonal vegetables, tossed in a light, flavorful dressing. A refreshing and healthy choice for any meal.

Ingredients

A fresh and vibrant mix of crisp lettuce, juicy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and seasonal vegetables, tossed in a light, flavorful dressing. A refreshing and healthy choice for any meal.

Steps

1. Wash and chop the vegetables. 2. Prepare the dressing by mixing olive oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper. 3. Toss the vegetables with the dressing until evenly coated. 4. Serve immediately or chill in the refrigerator for a refreshing salad.
